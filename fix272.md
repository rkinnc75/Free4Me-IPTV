> **Version:** patch on current `main` (**1.26.4+268**). Bump to **1.26.5+272**, tag `v1.26.5`.
> **Background (confirmed from the provider's get_live_streams dump):** the Z2U/barfik provider sends a `num` field (real provider order — already used by 'provider' mode) and injects ~910 "divider" entries whose names are fully wrapped in `#` (e.g. `##### KIDS NETWORK #####`). Those dividers have a stream_id but NO playable stream — clicking one buffers forever. Also, provider `num` order scatters same-category channels across the list (e.g. BEIN SPORTS 7 / MARQUEE stranded under the KIDS header).
> **Two additions (both per-source, opt-in, defaults unchanged):**
> 1. **'category' sort mode** — favorites first, then group by category (group_name), then provider order within the category. Reunites scattered same-category channels and moves stranded ones to their real category.
> 2. **hide_dividers toggle** — flags `#`-wrapped channels at import (`is_divider`) and filters them from browse views via an indexed WHERE.

## Verified (real sqlite_async + compile)
- Divider detection: `#`-wrapped names flagged (both insert paths); names merely containing `#` (e.g. `US (BTN+ 017)`) are NOT flagged. Persisted + round-tripped at column 20.
- Sort: provider vs category vs category+hide all produce correct output (category groups KIDS together, moves BEIN7/MARQUEE to SPORTS; hide removes the dead headers).
- `flutter analyze --no-fatal-infos` → 2 tolerated INFOs. Changelog matches `update_version_json.py`.
