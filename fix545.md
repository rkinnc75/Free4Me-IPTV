# fix545 — Live TV: "Favorites" replaces "All channels" as the rail top item; remove the standalone Favorites pill

**Base:** apply AFTER fix543 and fix544 (stacks on them; all touch `tv_guide_view.dart`).
**Version:** `2.0.44+544` → `2.0.45+545`
**Scope:** Live guide rail + pill removal. No schema/query change. Full suite green. Net code removal.

> ⚠️ Apply order: **fix543 → fix544 → fix545.** This patch is generated on top of fix544.

---

## Change (from device screenshot)

Live TV had two favorites controls: the category rail's top item said **"All channels"**, and a separate **"Favorites"** pill sat below the hero. Per the request, Live should match Movies/Series — Favorites as the rail's top item — and the redundant pill should go.

- The rail's top item is now **"Favorites"** (star icon) instead of "All channels". Selecting it shows live favorites (the all-channels view with the favorites filter on); selecting a category shows that category.
- Tapping the rail's Favorites item sets the favorites filter on; tapping a category turns it off (so the whole category shows — consistent with fix543).
- The **standalone Favorites pill is removed** (and its now-dead `_filterPills`/`_pill` helpers deleted).
- Default landing is unchanged (Favorites), and `reloadGuide()` (fix544) re-lands on Favorites.
