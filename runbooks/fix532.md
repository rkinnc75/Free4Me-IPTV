# fix532 — Raise Live TV category-rail cap 300 → 1000

**Base tag:** `v2.0.31` (branch fix branches from the most recent version tag, not `main`)
**Version:** `2.0.31+531` → `2.0.32+532`
**Scope:** one-line constant change in two TV view files + version bump + changelog. No schema, no query, no test changes.

---

## Problem

The Live TV category rail (both the TV "Live" browse grid and the TV guide) pages provider categories but stops at a hard ceiling of **300** (`_railCap`, introduced fix527). Providers with very large live-category lists are truncated: e.g. the Trex source in the field has **830 live categories**, so ~530 never appear at the bottom of the rail and are unreachable.

This is a soft display cap, unrelated to the separate "categories show but empty" (`cat_enabled`) and 89-second browse-hang issues — those are tracked separately and NOT addressed here.

## Fix

Raise `_railCap` from `300` to `1000` in both TV views that own the rail. The paging loop and `.take(_railCap)` truncation are otherwise unchanged, so the only effect is a higher ceiling. 1000 comfortably covers the largest observed provider (830) with headroom; the per-page `pageSize` paging and the `_itemCap`/`_channelCap` content guards are untouched.

Files:
- `lib/tv/tv_browse_view.dart` — `_railCap`
- `lib/tv/tv_guide_view.dart` — `_railCap`
