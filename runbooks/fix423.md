# fix423 — Wake the bottom control bar by tapping where it sits

**Target:** `1.35.22+423` (from `1.35.21+422`) · **Branch:** `main`

## What & why
In full-screen single-cell playback the control bar auto-hides (after
`devControlsHideSecs`, default 3 s). Tapping in the strip **where the bar
appears** did not bring it back — you had to tap higher up. Reported on live;
the same applied to VOD's bottom bar.

**Root cause (3 stacked factors):**
1. media_kit's show-on-tap `GestureDetector` is **hard-inset 16 px from every
   edge** (`media_kit_video` `material.dart`, `Positioned.fill(bottom:16…)`) — not themeable.
2. Free4Me's `_mpvThemeData` left `bottomButtonBarMargin` at its default
   (`bottom: 0`), so the 56 px bar renders **flush at the bottom edge**.
3. The app drives fullscreen itself (`handlesOwnFullscreen=false`, fix130), so
   media_kit applies **`EdgeInsets.zero`** instead of the system inset — and in
   immersive mode the bottom edge is also the **Android gesture-nav band**.

Net: the whole bottom-bar strip sits inside media_kit's tap-dead ring + the
system gesture band → taps there never reach the show-controls gesture. The
dead band is **device-dependent** (≈0 on TV/button-nav, ≈48 px on gesture-nav
phones), so the fix is **dynamic**.

## Fix
In `_mpvThemeData`, compute a dynamic `barLift` = `16 + max(systemGestureInsets.bottom,
viewPadding.bottom)`, clamped `[16, 96]`, and lift both the bottom button bar
(`bottomButtonBarMargin.bottom = barLift`) and the VOD seek bar
(`seekBarMargin.bottom = 60 + barLift`) above the dead band. Floor 16 covers
TVs (no insets; clears media_kit's ring); clamp guards absurd values.

where the bar was → controls should reappear. Confirm on a gesture-nav phone
(largest dead band) and the Shield.
