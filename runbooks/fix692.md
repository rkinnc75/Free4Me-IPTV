### version.json contract (compat-critical)
- **Legacy `apkUrl` now points at the UNIVERSAL APK** (was `-arm64.apk`).
  Pre-4.1.0 updaters only read `apkUrl`; universal installs on every device
  (fix504 history: arm64-only broke 32-bit boxes). They pay one larger
  download and are ABI-aware from then on.
- New `apkUrls` map: `{arm, arm64, x64, universal}`.
- New `apkSha256s` map + legacy `apkSha256` (=universal), injected by the CI
  post-upload sync step via `APK_SHA256_*` env vars; REMOVED on developer
  pre-tag runs (stale hashes would reject good downloads; missing hash =
  skip-verify, finding-151 semantics).
