## Why (reported on TV)
On TV, Sources → pencil (Edit) shows the source form but the bottom half (channel-order radios, hide-headers switch, source info rows, **Save/Cancel**) is off-screen and cannot be scrolled — only escape works. Cause in `edit_dialog.dart`: the layout was `Center → SingleChildScrollView → AlertDialog`. The scroll view wraps the *whole dialog box*, so the `AlertDialog` still sizes to its natural height and clips its own `content`; nothing inside scrolls. Fits on a phone, overflows the shorter usable height on TV.

## What this does
- Removes the outer `Center`/`SingleChildScrollView` wrapper.
- Returns the `AlertDialog` directly with `scrollable: true` (wires d-pad/keyboard scrolling of its own content + actions).
- Caps the content with `ConstrainedBox(maxHeight: screen − keyboard insets − 220)` so the dialog never grows past the viewport and the inner scroll engages.
- No model/DB/logic change; `maxContentHeight` is read by the `ConstrainedBox`.
