## Why (S24 screenshot, portrait 2x2 on ExoPlayer)
Video in multi-view cells was stretched to fill the cell (16:9 content crammed into a tall portrait cell). Cause: the cell hosts the engine view in `Stack(fit: StackFit.expand)` — TIGHT constraints. Under tight constraints Flutter's `AspectRatio` cannot choose its own size and is forced to fill, so the ratio is ignored. mpv's `Video` widget letterboxes internally, which is why only the ExoPlayer path stretched.

## What this does
- Wraps the Exo video in `ColoredBox(black) > Center > AspectRatio > VideoPlayer`. `Center` gives the `AspectRatio` loose constraints so it sizes to the correct ratio inside the cell; the black box provides proper letterbox bars. Applies to every Exo view (cells AND full-screen — full-screen already had a `Center` ancestor on some paths, now it is guaranteed regardless of host constraints).
- Kept separate from fix339 (independent rollback): 339 is a behaviour fix (restart loop), this is layout.
