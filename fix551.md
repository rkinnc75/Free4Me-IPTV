# fix551 — TV display polish: readable category tiles, search card sizing, lighter scrim

**Version:** 2.0.51 (`2.0.51+551`)
**Base:** v2.0.50 (fix550). Touches 4 TV/UI files, all disjoint from fix549 and
fix550 — verified clean standalone on v2.0.48 AND stacked after 549+550.
**Branch from:** latest version tag.

Bundles three independent TV display fixes (backlog #9/#10/#11). Pure
layout/value changes — no DB, no migration, no logic.

---

## #9 — Category tiles: 2-line label + wider tiles (8 → 6 across)

The category-selection tiles were too small to read at 10 feet (fix538 had
halved `maxCrossAxisExtent` 220→110 for density, which shrank/truncated labels).

- `tv_categories_view.dart`: `maxCrossAxisExtent` 110 → **150** (~8-across → ~6),
  `childAspectRatio` 0.82 → **0.74** (taller, room for a second text line).
- `channel_tile.dart` (poster body): label `maxLines` 1 → **2** with ellipsis,
  font 11 → 12. **Note:** the poster `ChannelTile` is shared, so movies/series
  poster tiles also gain 2-line labels — intended (more readable everywhere).

## #10 — TV search results: category-size cards, not phone-size

Search poster cards were smaller than the (now wider) category tiles and didn't
fit the new 2-line label.

- `tv_search_view.dart`: poster `rowHeight` 236 → **270**, `cardWidth` 150 →
  **168** so search poster cards match the category grid proportions and give the
  wrapped title room. Live (now/next) cards (320×104) unchanged.

## #11 — Background scrim: 90% → 80% (too dark)

- `tv_shell.dart`: scrim `Color(0xE6000000)` (~90%) → **`Color(0xCC000000)`**
  (~80%). Still legible over the neon art; lets more background glow through.

## NOT included — #12 (stale TV corner icon): needs confirmation

The TV top-left header icon in `tv_top_tab_bar.dart` **already references the
real app icon** (`assets/icon.png`, set in fix529 — "the real app icon, not the
placeholder green TV icon"). There is no stale in-app asset to swap.

If the icon still looks wrong on device, the likely culprit is the **Android
launcher icon** (`android/app/src/main/res/mipmap-*/ic_launcher*.webp`) not
matching `assets/icon.png` — a separate asset pipeline (regenerate launcher
icons from the source art). That can't be verified from code alone. **Held
pending a screenshot of what Rich is seeing** so we fix the right asset rather
than guess. Not in this runbook.
