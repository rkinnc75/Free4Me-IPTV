## Why (onn 4K Plus, Dino "Blood Diamond" via ExoPlayer)
The movie ran 3.5 min with audio but a black screen. Log: `ExoEngine: open() succeeded` + `initialised duration=8601s size=…` — the controller WAS live. Root cause in `exo_engine.dart`: `buildVideoView` returns a black `ColoredBox` until `controller.value.isInitialized`, but it is only called from `Player.build`, which rebuilds on its OWN setState (overlay/buffering). `open()` initializes the controller asynchronously AFTER the view is first built; on this box no setState landed once `isInitialized` flipped true, so the `VideoPlayer` widget was never mounted — audio played, screen stayed black. (mpv escapes this because its `Video` widget mounts immediately and attaches the texture internally; that is why earlier libmpv sessions on the same box rendered.)

## What this does
1. **Reactive view (fix):** `buildVideoView` now wraps the player in a `ValueListenableBuilder` on the controller (`VideoPlayerController` is a `ValueNotifier<VideoPlayerValue>`). The view rebuilds itself the moment initialization completes, independent of `Player.setState`, so `VideoPlayer` mounts as soon as the stream is ready.
2. **Diagnostic parity:** logs `ExoEngine: SURFACE[mid-playback+2s] initialized=… size=…` ~2s after init, mirroring the fix331 mpv probe, so any future ExoPlayer black-screen is traceable.
