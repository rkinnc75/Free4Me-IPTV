# fix329 — Show device name on the TV export QR portal (align with filenames) → v1.26.45+329

> ## WARNING BUILD MACHINE — APPLY THE PATCH, then RELEASE (EXACTLY). No questions, no DB to query.
> **FIRST:** `find . -name 'fix296-DIAGNOSE*.md' -delete 2>/dev/null || true`

## Why
Export filenames already carry a device tag (fix322), but the QR export portal page and the on-TV QR dialog just said "Free4Me-IPTV export" / "Download on your phone or PC" — no device identity. With multiple devices exporting to the same network this is ambiguous. Align the portal with the rest of the app.

## What this does
1. **`DeviceDetector.deviceLabel()` (new):** a human-readable device name (e.g. "Samsung SM-S938U", "onn. 4K Plus"), distinct from the filename-safe `deviceTag()`. De-dupes manufacturer when the model already starts with it; cached; empty on non-Android/failure.
2. **Portal header (export_server.dart):** `ExportServer` gains a `deviceName`; the index page shows "From **<device>**" under the title (HTML-escaped via a new `_esc`).
3. **QR dialog title (settings_view.dart):** reads "Download from <device>" when known, else the previous text.
4. `_runTvServerExport` resolves `deviceLabel()` and passes it through. No model/DB change.
