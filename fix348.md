# fix348 — Live TV control panel: remove non-functional skip buttons, keep play/pause → v1.27.0+348

## Why (feature list: "On Live TV determine if the control panel should display or not")
On the libmpv full-screen path, the seek bar and seek gestures were ALREADY hidden for live (`displaySeekBar`/`seekGesture`/`seekOnDoubleTap` gated on mediaType) — what remained was media_kit's default centre button row: **SkipPrevious / Play-Pause / SkipNext**. The skip buttons are playlist controls and do nothing on a single live stream — exactly the "if we cannot go back or forward, why have it" problem. The ExoPlayer live path already shows no transport (fix336 gates the VOD bar on duration > 0).

**Decision (the open item from the pending list):** keep the panel, remove only what doesn't work. The top bar (back/cast/PiP) and bottom bar (subtitles, audio, zoom, mini-player) are all useful on live; play/pause stays because mpv's cache makes pause-and-resume genuinely work on live. The alternative — a 300s DVR-to-disk buffer that would make live seeking real — is DEFERRED as a future feature, still on the pending list.

## What this does
- `_mpvThemeData` now overrides `primaryButtonBar`: **live** → centred play/pause only; **VOD/series** → the package default (skip buttons + play/pause), seek bar and gestures unchanged.
- No engine, settings, or DB changes.
